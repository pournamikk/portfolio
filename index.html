Mixed Methods · Quantitative Sep 2024

Customer Churn Analysis: The 28.1% Challenge

Led a mixed-methods investigation into a critical 28.1% user churn rate, deploying targeted HaTs surveys and in-app feedback tools across 2,900+ users. Conducted 1:1 deep-dive interviews with churned enterprise customers and journey-mapped the specific events leading to departure.

Primary driver identified: critical gaps in debugging visibility — users couldn't diagnose failed execution logs, creating dead ends. Brittle recovery experience drove abandonment, not feature lack.

Advocated for low-code features to reduce error surface. Drove multi-quarter roadmap pivot: redesigned execution logs, implemented in-context guidance, deployed churn monitoring dashboard.
Pillar Case Study
ResearchOps · System Design 2024 – Ongoing

Centralised Feedback: The Feed-bank System

Solved the silo problem — insights were fragmented across survey tools, support tickets, and sales notes, making longitudinal analysis impossible. Designed and architected the Feed-bank: a centralised UX research repository serving as a single source of truth for cross-functional BAP teams.

Shifted organisation from reactive fire-fighting to proactive, systematic evidence gathering. Insights now mapped directly to engineering prioritisation metrics, driving the product backlog.

System architecture: 5-stage pipeline — Structured Taxonomy → Multi-Channel Ingestion → Strategic Tagging → Data Governance → Quarterly Delivery to leadership. Implemented 12-week cooldown protocol and probability sampling to eliminate selection bias.
